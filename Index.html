ABOUT US

Welcome to the Anointed Church, a church of all nations where we focus on Jesus Christ the Author and Finisher of our faith. We believe that we are anointed to impact our world.

Our mission is to empower every member to develop an intimate relationship with God, develop a passion for souls, achieve God given potential, connect with other believers and to be a blessing in every area of life.

Our mandate is to raise a people of faith, perfect in their relationship with Jesus Christ.
